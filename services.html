Our Service

Responsible for recycling an important natural resource, of course. But, just as important, responsible for the job. Being there for you, when you need us, where you need us, without fail, with a program made to fit your company.

WE OFFER TWO ENVIRONMENTALLY FRIENDLY WOOD WASTE SERVICE OPTIONS:

1

Trailer Spot

For companies that can fill a trailer every two weeks, we’ll spot a semi at your dock, remove it when it’s full and replace it with an empty one with just 24-hours’ notice.

2

Live Load

We can also remove your wood waste with a live load. Simply call us and we’ll bring a trailer to your dock. Fill it up while we wait and then we’ll take it away.

Wood Recycling

We take clean wood waste. We can’t accept large bolts, foam from crates, plastic, shrink wrap, poly banding, metal banding, cardboard or any type of laminates. Our system can handle wood with small screws or nails.
