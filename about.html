About Us

Since 2001, we’ve worked with hundreds of Fortune 500 and mid-sized companies throughout Milwaukee and southeastern Wisconsin to provide reliable, turnkey and environmentally responsible wood waste disposal and recycling services.

We take wood pallets, crates, skids and scrap lumber off your floor. But, more importantly, we take it off your mind.

We are the largest wood recycler in the state and have more than 100 trailers to carry away your wood waste. Call us and we’ll be there as promised and on schedule. For many clients, we provide same day service.

We sort each load and repair and reuse pallets. Scrap wood and pallets that are badly damaged are converted to Enviro Mulch for wholesale distribution.

OUR COMMITMENT TO SUSTAINABILITY MEANS YOU CAN MEET YOUR ENVIRONMENTAL GOALS.

Our large fleet delivers 24-hour turnaround for you.

It’s simple. Every piece of wood that comes into our facility is put back into use.

We’ve diverted more than ONE BILLION POUNDS of wood from landfills.
